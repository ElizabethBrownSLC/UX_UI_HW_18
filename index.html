My Process

Research

Conducting research is a vital part of my process. The value of the information that can be obtained through user interactions equates to the success of the experience. My designs are user focused and research is where I get know what my users really want and need.

Ideation

I enjoy the ideation step in the UX process and find it very satisfying to be able to identify and define the users problems and then solve them through the process of ideation. This step is where my creativity is sparked and the information gathered through research fuels it.

Prototyping

Seeing the ideas generated to solve the users problems take shape through prototyping is my favorite part of UX/UI design. Conceptualizing the solutions generated through ideation is a rewarding exercise that provides a clear representation of how the user will interact with the experience.

Testing

Getting a clickable/working prototype into the hands of users gives and incredible amount of information. Even if the solution I have designed fails, I am so grateful for the opportunity to iterate on the design and create an even more delightful experience for the user.

The Future

The UX/UI process is one that needs to be revisited many times in the course of developing human-centric applications and experiences. I am always reminded how much I enjoy this process and why focusing on the user is my passion. Having completed work is always gratifying but the process is what I love.
